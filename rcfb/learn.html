What is a Food Bank?

🏪

A Food Bank is like a special store…

…where families can come to pick up healthy food for free when they really need it!

❓

Why do families need it?

Sometimes people don't have enough money to buy food. That doesn't mean anything is wrong with them — it can happen to anyone!

🤗

Who can come here?

Anyone who is hungry in Sacramento County. No ID, no proof of income, no zip code needed. You're always welcome!

🌟 Our motto: "Because No One Should Be Hungry"

⚙️

1

Who Do We Help?

Hunger can happen to anyone. We help all kinds of neighbors in Sacramento:

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Families

Parents and kids who need a helping hand getting food on the table

👴👵

Seniors

Older adults on fixed budgets who need nutritious meals

👷

Working Adults

People who work hard but still struggle to make ends meet

🌍

Everyone

Refugees, individuals with disabilities, anyone going through a tough time

❤️

We help everyone — no matter their race, age, or background. Everyone deserves food!
