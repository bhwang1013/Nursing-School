ICD-10 Codes – Common Categories

The International Classification of Diseases, 10th Revision (ICD-10), is used for diagnosing and billing. Each code maps to a specific condition.

CPT Codes – Common Nursing Procedures

Current Procedural Terminology (CPT) codes describe medical, surgical, and diagnostic services provided to patients.

NANDA Nursing Diagnoses

NANDA International nursing diagnoses are clinical judgments about individual, family, or community responses to actual or potential health problems.

Clinical Documentation Standards

Accurate documentation is critical to patient safety, care continuity, and legal compliance.

Hospital Emergency Codes

Standard color codes used in hospitals to communicate emergencies quickly and discretely.
