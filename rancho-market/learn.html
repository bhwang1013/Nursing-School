What is a Farmers' Market?

🚜

It's a market full of farmers!

Real farmers bring fruits, vegetables, honey, nuts, flowers, breakfast, lunch and more — all grown nearby!

✅

What does "Certified" mean?

Certified means every farmer here really grows what they sell. The State checks to make sure it's true!

🤗

Who can come?

Everyone is welcome! Families, friends, and neighbors come every Saturday to shop and have fun.

🌟 Open since 2010 — Rancho Cordova's only certified farmers' market!

⚙️
